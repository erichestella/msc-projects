Tribute for Im Na-yeon

A short tribute to honor her contributions in the field of music

Biography

Nayeon (Korean: 나연), born September 22, 1995, is a Korean singer-songwriter. Her real name is Im Na Yeon. She is the oldest member, lead singer and face of the girl group Twice. She grew up in Seoul, South Korea, where she lived with her parents and little sister Im Seo Yeon. As a child, she took part in a children's modeling competition. There she was discovered by JYP Entertainment and asked as a trainee. Her mother refused at the time, but 10 years later, in 2010, she secretly attended JYP's open casting and was accepted. Nayeon was selected for JYP's girl group 6mix, but the group never debuted. On May 5, 2015, she participated in JYP's survival show “SIXTEEN”. She made it to second place and became a member of the girl group TWICE.

Contributions:

  • TWICE’s Nayeon hit 200 million views on YouTube with "POP!", proving its lasting appeal. The song’s catchy melody and vibrant energy continue to shine.
  • She made a powerful comeback with her second mini-album "NA", led by the bold and trendy "ABCD". The track showcases her versatility and charm.
  • Her first mini-album IM NAYEON debuted at No. 7 on the Billboard 200, making her the first K-pop soloist to reach the top 10. With "NA", she earned her second consecutive top 10 spot.
  • The album debuted at No. 7 on the Billboard 200, following her first mini-album "IM NAYEON", marking her second consecutive top 10 entry and reinforcing her prowess as a solo artist.
  • Nayeon released her second mini-album, "NA", showcasing a different side of her artistry.
